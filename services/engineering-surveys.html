Precision Beyond the Blueprint

In the construction industry, even a few centimeters of error can lead to millions in rectification costs. GeoAxis Engineering specializes in **Construction Setting Out (Stakeout)**, ensuring that every pile, column, and road curb is positioned exactly as per the engineering design.

Our team works directly with Project Managers and Site Engineers to establish primary control points and secondary grid lines, facilitating seamless construction progress for both private developments and RDA-standard infrastructure projects.

Our Setting Out Methodology

A rigorous process to eliminate site discrepancies.

1

Design Verification (CAD)

We ingest your DWG/DXF architectural plans into our geomatics software, verifying coordinates against the site's established control network.

2

Primary Control Establishment

If not already present, we establish high-order site control stations using static GPS or closed-loop traverse methods to ensure long-term site stability.

3

Physical Stakeout & Marking

Our field team uses robotic Total Stations to mark design points on the ground using steel pins, nails, or paint, depending on the surface.

4

Confirmation & As-Built Report

Once staked, we record 'as-set' positions to provide a formal report to the client, confirming compliance with design tolerances.
