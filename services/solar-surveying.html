Our Assessment Process

Standardized technical evaluation for utility-scale solar.

1

Topographical Site Survey

We capture high-precision spot levels and contour data to identify undulating terrain that affects solar table mounting and drainage.

2

Digital Elevation Modeling (DEM)

Using drone photogrammetry or LiDAR, we create a 3D digital twin of the site to simulate sunlight throughout the year.

3

Sun-Path & Shading Simulation

Our engineers analyze potential shading from trees, buildings, or the terrain itself during different seasons to calculate effective sun-hours.

4

Final Deliverable & Design Specs

A comprehensive technical report including CAD files, slope maps, and shading profiles ready for system sizing and bankable audits.
