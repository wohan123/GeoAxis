Our Technical Workflow

How we ensure precision from field to floorplan.

1

Requirement Analysis

We verify your project goals, whether for a small residential plot or a multi-acre industrial site, ensuring we meet municipal council requirements.

2

Total Station & GPS Deployment

Our licensed surveyors use RTK-GPS for global positioning and high-precision Total Stations to capture exact spot levels across the terrain.

3

CAD Drafting & Quality Audit

Raw data is converted into detailed AutoCAD drawings, featuring contours, benchmarks, and architectural features.

4

Certified Deliverables

You receive a stamped survey plan and digital files (DWG, PDF) ready for your architectural design team.
