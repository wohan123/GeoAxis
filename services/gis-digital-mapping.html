Our GIS & Drone Mapping Workflow

Merging aerial precision with digital data science.

1

GCP Establishment

We place Ground Control Points (GCPs) surveyed with RTK-GPS to ensure the digital map is georeferenced to national coordinates (Kandawala Grid).

2

Autonomous Drone Flight

Deployment of industrial UAVs (DJI M300/M350) to capture high-overlap aerial imagery or LiDAR point clouds.

3

Photogrammetric Processing

Using Pix4D or Agisoft, we process thousands of images into seamless 2D orthomosaics and 3D mesh models.

4

GIS Integration & Analysis

Delivery of data in Shapefile (.shp), GeoTIFF, or online dashboard formats for direct use in ArcGIS or QGIS software.
